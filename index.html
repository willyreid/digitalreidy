Meet Digital Reidy

Hi, I'm Will Reid. With over 10 years in the software industry and 5 years working directly with small businesses, I know how daunting the digital world can feel — especially when you're trying to focus on actually running your business.

Digital Reidy exists to take that pressure off. Whether you're just starting out or looking to strengthen what you've already built, I'll guide you through the right steps at the right time — from business registration and domain names to email, websites, cybersecurity, and hardware.

No jargon. No upselling. Just straight-talking advice you can act on.
